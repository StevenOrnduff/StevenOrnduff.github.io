Shadows Below

https://linkthesm.itch.io/the-shadows-below

Shadows Bewlow was completed durring a 48 hour Game Jam with the Theme of Light and Shadow. The game is a stage based top down shooter. The goal is to gather Light balls and place them on the shadow spawners while fighting off shadow creatures. Once all the shadow spawners in the stage have been destroyed the path to the next stage opens. Each stage deeper the player goes the darker the stage gets. I used some assets form the Unity asset store to add some polish to the projcet.
